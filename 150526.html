Lopitalova teorema — sažeta rešenja

(a)

\[ \lim_{x\to 1} \tan\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)\ln x \]

Kako je \[ \tan\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right) = \frac{\sin\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)} {\cos\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)} \] i \[ \sin\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)\to 1, \] dovoljno je izračunati:

\[ \lim_{x\to 1} \frac{\ln x}{\cos\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)} \]

Dobijamo oblik \(0/0\), pa primenjujemo Lopitalovu teoremu:

\[ \begin{aligned} \lim_{x\to 1} \frac{\ln x}{\cos\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)} &= \lim_{x\to 1} \frac{\frac{1}{x}} {-\frac{\pi}{2}\sin\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)} \\[6pt] &= \frac{1}{-\frac{\pi}{2}} = -\frac{2}{\pi}. \end{aligned} \]

Odgovor:

\[ \boxed{-\frac{2}{\pi}} \]

(b)

\[ \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{2^x-1-x\ln 2}{3^x-1-x\ln 3} \]

Za \(x\to0\) imamo oblik \(0/0\). Primenjujemo Lopitalovu teoremu:

\[ \begin{aligned} \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{2^x-1-x\ln 2}{3^x-1-x\ln 3} &= \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{2^x\ln 2-\ln 2}{3^x\ln 3-\ln 3} \\[6pt] &= \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{(\ln 2)(2^x-1)}{(\ln 3)(3^x-1)} \\[6pt] &= \frac{\ln 2}{\ln 3} \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{2^x-1}{3^x-1}. \end{aligned} \]

Opet imamo oblik \(0/0\), pa još jednom primenjujemo Lopitalovu teoremu:

\[ \begin{aligned} \frac{\ln 2}{\ln 3} \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{2^x-1}{3^x-1} &= \frac{\ln 2}{\ln 3} \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{2^x\ln 2}{3^x\ln 3} \\[6pt] &= \frac{\ln 2}{\ln 3}\cdot \frac{\ln 2}{\ln 3}. \end{aligned} \]

Odgovor:

\[ \boxed{\left(\frac{\ln 2}{\ln 3}\right)^2} \]

(c)

\[ \lim_{x\to 1^+} \frac{\ln(\ln x)}{\ln(x-1)} \]

Kada \(x\to1^+\), važi: \[ \ln x\to0^+, \qquad x-1\to0^+. \] Zato je oblik \(-\infty/-\infty\).

Primenjujemo Lopitalovu teoremu:

\[ \begin{aligned} \lim_{x\to 1^+} \frac{\ln(\ln x)}{\ln(x-1)} &= \lim_{x\to 1^+} \frac{\frac{1}{x\ln x}}{\frac{1}{x-1}} \\[6pt] &= \lim_{x\to 1^+} \frac{x-1}{x\ln x} \\[6pt] &= \lim_{x\to 1^+} \frac{1}{x}\cdot\frac{x-1}{\ln x} \\[6pt] &= 1\cdot1 = 1. \end{aligned} \]

Odgovor:

\[ \boxed{1} \]

(d)

\[ \lim_{x\to+\infty} x\left(\arctan x-\frac{\pi}{2}\right) \]

Oblik je \(\infty\cdot0\), pa izraz prebacujemo u količnik:

\[ x\left(\arctan x-\frac{\pi}{2}\right) = \frac{\arctan x-\frac{\pi}{2}}{\frac{1}{x}}. \]

Sada imamo oblik \(0/0\), pa primenjujemo Lopitalovu teoremu:

\[ \begin{aligned} \lim_{x\to+\infty} \frac{\arctan x-\frac{\pi}{2}}{\frac{1}{x}} &= \lim_{x\to+\infty} \frac{\frac{1}{1+x^2}}{-\frac{1}{x^2}} \\[6pt] &= \lim_{x\to+\infty} \left(-\frac{x^2}{1+x^2}\right) \\[6pt] &= -1. \end{aligned} \]

Odgovor:

\[ \boxed{-1} \]
