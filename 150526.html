Lopitalova teorema — sažeta rešenja

(a)

\[ \lim_{x\to 1}\tan\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)\ln x \]

Pošto je \[ \tan\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right) = \frac{\sin\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)} {\cos\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)}, \] a \[ \sin\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)\to 1, \] dovoljno je posmatrati:

\[ \lim_{x\to1}\frac{\ln x}{\cos\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)} \]

Ovo je oblik \(0/0\), pa primenjujemo Lopitala:

\[ \lim_{x\to1} \frac{\frac1x} {-\frac{\pi}{2}\sin\left(\frac{\pi x}{2}\right)} = \frac{1}{-\frac{\pi}{2}} = -\frac{2}{\pi} \]

Odgovor:

\[ \boxed{-\frac{2}{\pi}} \]

(b)

\[ \lim_{x\to0} \frac{2^x-1-\ln(2)x}{3^x-1-\ln(3)x} \]

Za \(x\to0\) imamo oblik \(0/0\). Prvi Lopital:

\[ \lim_{x\to0} \frac{2^x\ln2-\ln2}{3^x\ln3-\ln3} = \lim_{x\to0} \frac{\ln2(2^x-1)}{\ln3(3^x-1)} \] \[ = \frac{\ln2}{\ln3} \lim_{x\to0} \frac{2^x-1}{3^x-1} \]

Opet imamo oblik \(0/0\), pa još jednom Lopital:

\[ = \frac{\ln2}{\ln3} \lim_{x\to0} \frac{2^x\ln2}{3^x\ln3} = \frac{\ln2}{\ln3}\cdot\frac{\ln2}{\ln3} \]

Odgovor:

\[ \boxed{\left(\frac{\ln2}{\ln3}\right)^2} \]

(c)

\[ \lim_{x\to1+0} \frac{\ln(\ln x)}{\ln(x-1)} \]

Kada \(x\to1+\), važi: \[ \ln x\to0^+,\qquad x-1\to0^+. \] Zato je: \[ \ln(\ln x)\to-\infty,\qquad \ln(x-1)\to-\infty. \] Imamo oblik \(-\infty/-\infty\), pa primenjujemo Lopitala.

\[ \lim_{x\to1+} \frac{\ln(\ln x)}{\ln(x-1)} = \lim_{x\to1+} \frac{\frac{1}{x\ln x}}{\frac{1}{x-1}} \] \[ = \lim_{x\to1+} \frac{x-1}{x\ln x} = \lim_{x\to1+} \frac{1}{x}\cdot\frac{x-1}{\ln x} \] \[ = 1\cdot1=1 \]

Odgovor:

\[ \boxed{1} \]

(d)

\[ \lim_{x\to+\infty} x\left(\arctan x-\frac{\pi}{2}\right) \]

Ovo je oblik \(\infty\cdot0\). Prebacujemo u količnik:

\[ x\left(\arctan x-\frac{\pi}{2}\right) = \frac{\arctan x-\frac{\pi}{2}}{\frac1x} \]

Sada imamo oblik \(0/0\), pa primenjujemo Lopitala:

\[ \lim_{x\to+\infty} \frac{\arctan x-\frac{\pi}{2}}{\frac1x} = \lim_{x\to+\infty} \frac{\frac{1}{1+x^2}}{-\frac{1}{x^2}} \] \[ = \lim_{x\to+\infty} -\frac{x^2}{1+x^2} = -1 \]

Odgovor:

\[ \boxed{-1} \]
