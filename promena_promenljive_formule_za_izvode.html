Promena nezavisne promenljive

Polazna smena

\[ x=\varphi(t),\qquad y(x)=Y(t),\qquad \varphi'(t)\ne0. \]

Prvi izvod

\[ y'=\frac{dy}{dx} =\frac{\dfrac{dY}{dt}}{\dfrac{dx}{dt}} =\boxed{\frac{Y'}{\varphi'}}. \]

Drugi izvod

\[ \begin{aligned} y'' &=\frac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{Y'}{\varphi'}\right) =\frac1{\varphi'}\frac{d}{dt}\left(\frac{Y'}{\varphi'}\right)\\[2mm] &=\frac1{\varphi'} \cdot \frac{Y''\varphi'-Y'\varphi''}{(\varphi')^2}\\[2mm] &=\boxed{ \frac{Y''\varphi'-Y'\varphi''}{(\varphi')^3} }. \end{aligned} \]

Primena na Ojlerovu smenu

\[ t=\ln x \quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad x=e^t=\varphi(t), \] \[ \varphi'=e^t=x,\qquad \varphi''=e^t=x. \]
\[ \boxed{y'=\frac{Y'}{x}}, \qquad \boxed{y''=\frac{Y''-Y'}{x^2}}. \] \[ \boxed{xy'=Y'}, \qquad \boxed{x^2y''=Y''-Y'}. \]
