Zadatak 16.1.

Mešoviti razlomci, decimalni brojevi i periodični zapis.

Zadatak

Odrediti vrednost izraza:

\[ \frac{ \left(2.4+1\frac12\right)\cdot 2.5+ \left(6\frac{1}{12}:6-1\frac{1}{72}\right): \left(8\frac57-1\frac{5}{21}\right) }{ 0.4\cdot \left(54.75-4.5:0.1\right)\cdot \left(3\frac12+0.666\ldots\right)\cdot \frac35 }. \]

Ideja

Sve pretvaramo u razlomke. Posebno važno:

\[ 0.666\ldots=\frac23. \]

Zadatak izgleda dugačak, ali se jedan deo brojioca poništava na nulu.

Prebacivanje u razlomke

Zapis Razlomak
\(2.4\) \(\frac{24}{10}=\frac{12}{5}\)
\(2.5\) \(\frac52\)
\(0.4\) \(\frac25\)
\(4.5\) \(\frac92\)
\(0.1\) \(\frac1{10}\)
\(54.75\) \(\frac{5475}{100}=\frac{219}{4}\)
\(0.666\ldots\) \(\frac23\)

Brojilac

Prvi deo brojioca:

\[ \left(2.4+1\frac12\right)\cdot 2.5 = \left(\frac{12}{5}+\frac32\right)\cdot \frac52 = \left(\frac{24}{10}+\frac{15}{10}\right)\cdot \frac52 = \frac{39}{10}\cdot \frac52 = \frac{39}{4}. \]

Drugi deo brojioca:

\[ 6\frac{1}{12}:6-1\frac{1}{72} = \frac{73}{12}:6-\frac{73}{72} = \frac{73}{12}\cdot \frac16-\frac{73}{72} = \frac{73}{72}-\frac{73}{72} = 0. \]

Zato je ceo drugi sabirak u brojiocu jednak nuli:

\[ \left(6\frac{1}{12}:6-1\frac{1}{72}\right): \left(8\frac57-1\frac{5}{21}\right) = 0. \]

Dakle, brojilac je:

\[ B=\frac{39}{4}. \]

Imenilac

Najpre sredimo zagrade:

\[ 54.75-4.5:0.1 = \frac{219}{4}-\frac92:\frac1{10} = \frac{219}{4}-\frac92\cdot 10 = \frac{219}{4}-45 = \frac{219}{4}-\frac{180}{4} = \frac{39}{4}. \]
\[ 3\frac12+0.666\ldots = \frac72+\frac23 = \frac{21}{6}+\frac{4}{6} = \frac{25}{6}. \]

Sada imenilac iznosi:

\[ I= \frac25\cdot \frac{39}{4}\cdot \frac{25}{6}\cdot \frac35. \]

Skratimo:

\[ I= \frac25\cdot \frac{39}{4}\cdot \frac{25}{6}\cdot \frac35 = \frac{39}{4}. \]

Konačan odgovor

\[ \frac{B}{I} = \frac{\frac{39}{4}}{\frac{39}{4}} = 1. \]
\[ \boxed{1} \]

Napomena

Ovde je korisno prvo uočiti da je \[ 6\frac{1}{12}:6=1\frac{1}{72}, \] pa je taj deo brojioca odmah jednak nuli. To značajno skraćuje račun.
